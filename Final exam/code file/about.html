About Department

Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is one of the major academic units of the State University of Bangladesh. It plays a vital role in producing IT professionals to serve the new and fast-growing knowledge-based industry. The Department offers B.Sc. in CSE (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering) program. Courses are conducted utilizing advanced technology with an emphasis on hands-on-training. With academicians with excellent work experience and impressive credentials, the students are assured of high quality education, suited for the evolving era. The academicians prepare students to meet the challenge of a global work environment as well as the shortfall of an emerging ICT sector in Bangladesh. Class lectures focus on real-life problem solving as used in the industry with an underlying emphasis on the foundational aspects of CSE.
